About the Firm

A Legacy of Legal Excellence

Kariuki & Associates was founded with a singular mission — to provide Kenyans with access to the highest quality legal representation, regardless of the complexity of their matter.

With over two decades of practice across Nairobi's High Court, Court of Appeal, and Supreme Court, our team has built a reputation for meticulous preparation, strategic thinking, and unwavering advocacy. We do not take every case. We take the right cases, and we fight for them completely.
